SKAI & KOJF

SKAI & KOJF offers training in traditional Shotokan Karate-do, which emphasizes strong stances & precise techniques, Practice consists of training in Kihon (basics),Kata(forms),Kumite (sparring).


OUR MISSION

SKAI & KOJF offers a structured program Where each individual can experience and learn self-esteem, respect, loyalty, humility, courage, sportsmanship and teamwork in a fun, safe and nurturing environment where everyone is a winner.

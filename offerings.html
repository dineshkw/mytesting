Our Offerings

Classes : Group , Individual , Institutions & Schools

We teach traditional Shotokan Karate for students of age above 5. We have specify methods and training for Kids, Men & Women. We also specially teach for senior black belters The Shitoryu style of karate-do.

Technical Seminar :

Includes kihon, kata, kumite and advance skills. This attracts students from all over the country.

Technical Clinic - Referee (Kata & Kumite) :

We provide opportunity for our senior members to take up examination for Judge & Referee (According to World Karate do Federation Rules & Regulations).

Championship :

Our annual association championship is open for all our students. We also invited to take part in District, Provincial, National & International Championships.

Our Certified Kyu Grading Test :

Requirements are physical fitness, kihon, association syllabus, kata, kumite & attendance.

Our Certified Dan Grading Test :

Requirements are physical fitness, kihon, association syllabus, kata, kumite, oral test & attendance. Also important is the proper duration between Dan levels.
